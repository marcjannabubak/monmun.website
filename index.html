What is Model United Nations?

Model United Nations (MUN) is a student simulation of the United Nations, where participants take on the roles of international delegates to debate pressing global issues, negotiate with peers, and draft real policy resolutions. It's an experience that builds public speaking, critical thinking, and creates an educational, but also fun enviornment.

Why choose MonMUN?

As a first edition conference, MONMUN strives to bring together students who are eager to broaden their horizons, challenge their perspectives, and make lasting connections along the way. We believe that great diplomacy doesn't have to come at the expense of a great time so expect meaningful debate, a welcoming atmosphere, and plenty of memorable moments both inside and outside the committee room.
